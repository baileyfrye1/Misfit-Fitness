Meet Stephanie

I proudly coach clients of all ages, helping them transform their lives

I've been an athlete at heart since I was a kid, loving the outdoors and playing sports from the age of 10. Now, my focus is on bodybuilding, and I've been training since 2013. Starting as a coach for high school girls, I'm passionate about showing that women can excel in weightlifting just as much as men do. As a NASM certified trainer, I proudly coach clients of all ages, from young to 70+, helping them transform their lives. It's not just about losing weight; it's about embracing a healthier, stronger lifestyle both in and out of the gym. I'm always learning to spread my love for this journey. Let me use my experience and knowledge to help you make positive changes!
